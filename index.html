Privacy Policy for DarkPDF

Effective Date: 11/15/2024

Overview

DarkPDF respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This privacy policy outlines the types of information we do and do not collect, and how it is used.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • Personal Data: This extension does not collect, store, or share any personal information such as names, email addresses, or payment details.
  • Usage Data: We do not track or analyze your usage of the extension or its features.
  • Third-Party Data: The extension does not send any data to third-party services or external servers.

Information We May Store Locally

User Preferences: To enhance your experience, the extension may store settings (e.g., your selected dark mode) locally on your device. This data is never transmitted or shared externally.

Third-Party Services

This extension does not integrate with or rely on any third-party services, APIs, or trackers.
